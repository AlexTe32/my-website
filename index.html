HELLO WORLD

I've been passionate about computers and video games since I was a child. That early interest made me curious about how games and programs work, and it pushed me to start learning on my own.

During my teenage years, I taught myself Python, focusing on basic programming concepts, game development, and small automation projects. Coding quickly became one of my favorite ways to be creative and solve problems.

My passion for technology guided my education. I chose to study at the Regional Center for Vocational Education and Training “Moša Pijade” in Tetovo, North Macedonia, where I completed my secondary education as an Electrical Technician for Computer Technology and Automation.

Currently, I’m pursuing a degree in Software Engineering at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering in Skopje. In my free time, I enjoy joining game jams and working on side projects that help me improve my skills and explore new ideas.
